# Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

## Introduction

Siinc Pty Ltd ("Siinc", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our backup and data protection service for Autodesk Construction Cloud ("Service").

By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

When you register for or use our Service, we may collect:

- **Account Information**: Name, email address, company name, job title
- **Billing Information**: Payment card details, billing address (processed securely through our payment providers)
- **ACC Integration Data**: OAuth tokens and permissions required to access your Autodesk Construction Cloud account
- **Support Communications**: Information you provide when contacting our support team

### Information Collected Automatically

When you use our Service, we automatically collect:

- **Usage Data**: Features used, backup schedules, restore activities, storage volumes
- **Log Data**: IP address, browser type, operating system, access times, pages viewed
- **Device Information**: Hardware model, operating system version, unique device identifiers

### Backup Data

- We access and store backup copies of your ACC project data as directed by you
- This data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
- We do not analyze or access the content of your backed-up data except as necessary to provide the Service or as required by law

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- **Provide the Service**: Execute backups, enable restores, maintain data integrity
- **Manage Your Account**: Authentication, billing, account administration
- **Improve Our Service**: Analyze usage patterns, optimize performance, develop new features
- **Communicate**: Send service updates, security alerts, support responses
- **Ensure Security**: Detect and prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and other security threats
- **Comply with Legal Obligations**: Meet regulatory requirements, respond to legal requests

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Locations

- Primary data storage in your selected region (US, EU, AU, or custom)
- Data may be processed in Australia, United States, or other countries where our service providers operate

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- AES-256 encryption for data at rest
- TLS 1.3 for data in transit
- Multi-factor authentication options
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Strict access controls and monitoring

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## Data Retention

- **Account Data**: Retained while your account is active and for 30 days after closure
- **Backup Data**: Retained according to your configured retention policies
- **Logs**: Generally retained for 90 days for security and debugging purposes
- **Billing Records**: Retained as required by tax and accounting regulations (typically 7 years)
